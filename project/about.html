About Us

We Are a mobile car detailing company based out of Las Vegas, NV

Mobile car detailing is a convenient service where professional detailers come to your location to clean and restore your vehicle. This service typically includes exterior washing, waxing, interior vacuuming, upholstery cleaning, and polishing, using specialized tools and products. Mobile car detailing offers the advantage of saving time and effort, as it eliminates the need to drive to a detailing shop. Additionally, it allows for personalized, on-site care tailored to your specific needs and preferences.


Mobile Car Detailing:

Exterior Cleaning: The car is washed thoroughly using specialized cleaning products to remove dirt and grime. The wheels, tires, and wheel wells are cleaned separately with brushes and appropriate cleaners. The car is then rinsed to remove all soap and contaminants.


Claying, Polishing, Waxing, and Sealing:

A clay bar is used to remove embedded contaminants from the car's paint surface, ensuring a smooth finish. The paint is polished to remove minor scratches, swirl marks, and oxidation, enhancing the carâ€™s shine. A wax or paint sealant is applied to protect the paint and give it a glossy finish.


Interior Cleaning and Detailing:

The interior is vacuumed thoroughly, including seats, carpets, and mats. Upholstery and leather seats are cleaned and conditioned. All surfaces, including the dashboard, console, and door panels, are wiped down and treated with appropriate cleaners. Interior crevices and vents are cleaned with brushes and compressed air. Windows and mirrors are cleaned inside and out for streak-free clarity.
