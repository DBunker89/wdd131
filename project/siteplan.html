Overview

Site Name

I chose to call this site Jackpot Detailing. I chose this because I am based out of Las Vegas where I was born and raised. Las Vegas's claim to fame is gambling, so I chose to name it Jackpot Detailing.

Purpose

Create a website to promote a mobile car detailing company called "Jackpot Detailing" to get more customers.

Scenerios

For Jackpot Detailing

What is the best day to book your company to come to my home to detail my cars?

If it rains the very next day after you come out to detail my car, would you provide a discount on my next wash?
